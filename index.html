About Carson

My name is Carson Kirby. I am from Edgewood, a small town in northeast Iowa. My family includes my parents; Wayne and Tammy, and my 3 younger sisters; Campbell, Lauren, and Faith. Our dog, Axl rounds out my family.

I am currently a third year student at the University of Iowa studying Business Analytics and Information Systems. At the University of Iowa, I am also involved in the Hawkeye Marching Band and Iowa Pep Band. Being involved in these groups has given me the opportunity to perform at many exciting athletic events in Iowa City and around the country.

Growing up in Iowa, I have been an avid fan of the Iowa Hawkeyes from a young age. I have always loved sporting the black and gold to watch the Hawkeyes play. As a kid, my bedroom was decorated from floor to ceiling in Iowa Hawkeye decor.
